AQUALUNG

Aqualung, the next chapter of autonomous submarine revolution , is going to be calculated and
designed rectangular vehicle. The laws of mechanics , motion , fluid and thermo dynamics are
main focus to design the shape of our final phase.
Aqualung is designed to perform all the picking dropping surfing tasks in the smoothest manner.
Things to be bold our bot isn’t remote control and its algorithm isn’t like other ordinary line
following robots. It is rather a highly advanced coded object recognition and decision making AI
by our software team that makes it so special. The hardware department is focusing on pulling
the maximum output from the bot. In the test phase we are using DC motors with brilliant
waterproofing technique but it significantly needed to be changed with brushless motors in final
phase . Talking on motors, it has 7 motors and propellers. Seven motors are for controlling and
one is for boosting speed.
